About Screen Resolution

An online tool to check the screen resolution of any display device. Test the resolution of your monitor, iPad, tablet, iPhone, MacBook, or any other display device. Screen resolution is the number of pixels in the x and y dimensions. It is generally measured as width x height in pixels.

Your current screen resolution may be less than the maximum supported screen resolution. You can change your screen resolution from your device settings.
